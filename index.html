Econ Quiz

Question #1: When there are unlimited wants, but limited resources, this is called...

scarcity.

Question #2: When you are not using all of your resources to their full potential, this is called...

underutilization.

Question #3:Wants are...

unlimited and always changing.

Question #4:The "Four Factors of Production" are...

land, labor, capital, entrepreneurship.

Question #5:The study of choosing scarce resources to satisfy wants:

Economics

Question #6:Adam Smith published a book called...,but called it...

The Wealth of Nations, "The Invisible Hand"
